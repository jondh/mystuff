Lines
Construction Status: Planning
Summary. Lines is for seeing your lines. Going for a run. Sure. Commuting to work. Yeah. Just walking around. Of course. These are all lines.
Phase 1: Record lines. Show lines according to date and time. Display lines on a map view.
Cross-platform: ReactNative or Flutter. Store line data on Firebase. Auth using Firebase.
Photography App
Construction Status: Framed
Summary. Display photographs in different collections. Show photographs in different frames. Support showing in AR.
Phase 1: Store and display photographs and collections. Show frame options around photographs.
Phase 2: Support showing photos w/ frames in AR. Includes different sizes for photographs.
Phase 3: Build out flow for ordering and shipping (and payment) for product.
Native Android. AWS S3 for photo storage. Firebase for data storage. Python for processing tasks.
Bartender Help
Construction Status: Prototype w/ data
Summary. Be a better bartender. Learn your drink. Learn how to pour.
Phase 1: Display different drinks and steps to make them
Phase 2: Simulate pouring / mixing drinks into a glass
Phase 3: Link to history / information for drinks and liquers
Native Android. Maybe migrate to cross-platform
